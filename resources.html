Resources Hub

While Project Zion provides immediate, anonymous support, we recognize that some situations require specialized assistance from established organizations and professionals. Below is a carefully curated list of trusted resources to help you navigate various aspects of digital safety, cybercrime recovery, and personal well-being.

üìû Emergency Resources

If you are in immediate physical danger, call your local emergency services:

  • India: 100 (Police), 108 (Emergency Services), 1930 (Cyber Crime)
  • US: 911
  • UK: 999
  • EU: 112

For immediate mental health crisis support, contact your local crisis hotline or emergency mental health services.

üì± Immediate Protection Tools

While seeking help, these tools can provide immediate protection:

Password & Account Security

  • Password Managers: Use tools like Bitwarden, 1Password, or LastPass
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Enable 2FA on all critical accounts
  • Account Recovery: Update recovery information for all accounts
  • Privacy Settings: Review and tighten privacy settings on social media
